What do we do?

We use Doolitle method to find LU matrices. After that we solve system of equations in form Ly=Ci and Uxi=y where xi is a vector from column of inversed matrix and Ci is a vector from column of Identity matrix. That's how we find A inversed. Computational complexicity for this method is O(n^2)
